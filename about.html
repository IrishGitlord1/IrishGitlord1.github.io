About

We the Kempal Development team have made this app in order to assist students in chemistry classes all around the world who have issues with remembering the order of the elements on the periodic table, putting the Atomic Symbol to the Element on the Periodic Table, figuring out the Atomic Density of elements, etc. This app is a non-profit resource for students all over the world who experience issues with this subject, and grants them an easy-to-use interface and readable graphics to give them knowledge and access to information on topics they struggle on.
This app was finished on: 10/ /2019 Lead Coder/ Team Manager: Kristopher Ireland Lead Researcher/ Secondary Coder : Xander Faircloth Lead Designer/ Writer: Dylan Hayes Secondary Coder/Secondary Designer: Alex Prinzo
